July 2019

The summer we met! Dawson had an internship in STL and came to my home church, Ches Pres, while there for the summer. He got to know the college-aged crowd very well before I ever met him. We actually met because he showed up uninvited to my high school graduation party with my church friends! What followed was a couple months of hanging out with our friends from church and getting to know each other. We went to Elephant Rocks State Park with our friends at the end of summer, and Dawson mustered up the courage to ask for us to take a picture together…the official first picture! Although the connection had sparked, we said our goodbyes knowing we were in very different stages and very different locations! (Indiana & Oklahoma)

June 2020

We reconnected after a school year of no conversation during COVID quarantine by catching up over Facetime. Then he came back to STL for another summer internship, and I was excited to spend this day with my friend, and only a friendship is where I wanted to keep it for some time. Dawson taught me how to mountain bike that day, we paddle boarded on this lake, and then he had prepared a picnic on the sand with my favorite snack included, Cheddar Cheese Pretzel Sandwiches! I was still convinced we were just friends reconnecting, and I believed that for a lot of the 2020 summer.

July 2020

I worked at camp during the month of June and early July, and I was still unsure where Dawson and I were at. On this day though, we cast our worries about the future aside to enjoy the beautiful summer day biking the Katy Trail and making a couple pit-stops along the way.

  • Next

August 2020

My last day in STL before returning to Tulsa. Dawson officially asked me out on a real date this night and I said yes. He picked out a restaurant in Lafayette Square and we walked around the park and ate ice cream afterwards. I still wasn’t ready to make things official, but Dawson was always very patient to wait and give me the time I needed. Looking back, we were thankful to have had this summer of getting to know each other better and building our friendship.

November 2020

I came to visit Dawson at his school in Terre Haute, IN at the beginning of Thanksgiving Break after a semester of Facetiming. Somehow we didn’t get a picture together this weekend, so here is a shot Dawson got of me tired of my school work. And we finally made things official!
